About Blue Lodge

Blue Lodge Memphis is a beautiful 3 bedroom bed and breakfast nestled in the Tenessee mountains in the outskirts of Memphis. You can wake up to a stunning view of the Blue Ridge Mountains and sip your complemetary coffee while your eating breakfast on the deck. You can reserve a room for $100 a night or rent the whole house for $250 a night. You can book a room by going to the contact us page.
